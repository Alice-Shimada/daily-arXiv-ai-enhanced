Method: 直接利用光谱像素作为维度，应用t-SNE降维算法生成投影图，并基于参考星表校准光谱相似性度量标准，从而筛选出大气参数相近（Teff ±200 K, logg ±0.3 dex, [Fe/H] ±0.2 dex）的恒星样本。

precise results in the spectroscopic analyses of F-, G-, and K-type stars.

effective temperatures, surface gravities, and metallicities, respectively. We
achieved completeness between 74-98 % and typical purity between 39-54 % in
this selection. With this, we will drastically facilitate the detection of
stars with similar spectra for a successful differential analysis. We used this
method to evaluate the accuracy and precision of four atmospheric parameter

analysis methods needs improvement.
