### [1] [Comparing Hemispheres: Anisotropy in the deceleration parameter $q_0$](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.03004)
*Mauricio Lopez-Hernandez,Josue De-Santiago*

Main category: astro-ph.CO

TL;DR: 本文利用Pantheon+ Ia型超新星样本对减速参数q₀进行半球对比分析，检验宇宙加速膨胀的各向同性及红移校正的鲁棒性；发现q₀存在方向性变化，即使在已校正CMB偶极和本动速度的z_HD红移系中仍残留偶极各向异性（Δq₀=0.112，S/N=2.155），其方向与CMB偶极一致；由超新星数据反推的太阳运动速度与Planck CMB偶极存在约1.9σ偏差；若将该SNe推得的偶极纳入红移校正流程，各向异性显著减弱，表明当前基于密度场重建的本动速度模型未能完全刻画局域大尺度流，构成低红移超新星宇宙学的重要系统误差源。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 检验宇宙加速膨胀是否具有方向依赖性（即各向同性假设是否成立），并评估当前红移校正（特别是CMB偶极和本动速度修正）在超新星宇宙学中的完备性与鲁棒性。

Method: 基于Pantheon+ Ia型超新星样本，在不同红移框架（z_hel、z_CMB、z_HD）下进行半球比较分析，拟合减速参数q₀的方向依赖性；通过偶极拟合反推太阳系相对于静止宇宙参考系的速度；将反推的偶极引入红移校正流程，检验各向异性是否被抑制。

Result: 在z_HD框架中仍观测到显著偶极各向异性（Δq₀=0.112，S/N=2.155），方向与CMB偶极一致；由SNe数据推得的偶极速度v_⊙=307.26^{+32.00}_{-22.28} km/s，方向与Planck结果存在~1.9σ偏差；引入该偶极校正后，q₀各向异性大幅削弱（S/N ≤ 1.75），剩余涨落与统计噪声一致。

Conclusion: 当前基于密度场的本动速度重建模型未能完全捕捉局域残余大尺度流，导致红移校正不完善，构成低红移Ia型超新星宇宙学中不可忽略的系统误差源；宇宙加速的各向异性信号部分源于速度场建模缺陷，而非新物理。

Abstract: We present a hemispherical comparison analysis of the deceleration parameter $q_0$ using the Pantheon+ sample of Type Ia supernovae to test the isotropy of cosmic acceleration and the robustness of redshift corrections. We detect directional variations in $q_0$ across redshift frames. Even in the $z_{\mathrm{HD}}$ frame, where corrections for the CMB dipole and peculiar velocities are applied, a residual dipolar anisotropy persists with $Δq_0 = 0.112$ and a maximum signal to noise $S/N = 2.155$, aligned with the CMB dipole direction and decreasing with increasing minimum redshift cut. The anisotropy is stronger in the $z_{\mathrm{hel}}$ and $z_{\mathrm{CMB}}$ frames, where kinematic corrections are incomplete, while the transition to $z_{\mathrm{HD}}$ reduces but does not remove the signal. Inferring the dipole from the supernovae data yields $v_{\odot} = 307.26^{+32.00}_{-22.28},\mathrm{km \, s^{-1}}$ toward $(\mathrm{RA},\mathrm{DEC}) = (156.40^{+4.72}_{-4.71}, -3.38^{+5.54}_{-8.23})^\circ$, mildly discrepant with the Planck CMB dipole at the $\sim 1.9σ$ level. When this SNe inferred dipole is incorporated into the redshift correction pipeline, the hemispherical anisotropy is suppressed, with the dipolar pattern disappearing and the maximum signal reduced to $S/N \lesssim 1.75$, while the remaining fluctuations become consistent with statistical noise, suggesting that part of the signal arises from residual mismatches in the modeling of the local velocity field. Since current redshift corrections rely on peculiar velocity reconstructions based on the density field, our results suggest a residual bulk flow not fully captured by these models, highlighting a source of systematic uncertainty in low redshift supernova cosmology.

### [2] [Post-Recombination Fluctuations from a Sequestered Dark Sector](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.03007)
*Salvatore Bottaro,Michael Geller,Diego Redigolo,Maya Tsur*

Main category: astro-ph.CO

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种形式化方法，用于刻画晚期宇宙扰动源在短于视界时间尺度内注入时留下的印记，并重点研究了再复合后注入对CMB的影响，特别是通过ISW效应；结果表明各向异性应力的瞬时注入起主导作用，并将该框架应用于隔离暗区的一阶相变，得出当前观测对能量注入分数的限制达千分之一量级。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 理解晚期宇宙中短时标能量注入对宇宙微波背景（CMB）等观测信号的影响，尤其缺乏对一般性扰动类型（标量、矢量、张量）下光子测地线修正的系统性处理。

Method: 基于注入时间远小于哈勃时间的假设，推导再复合后标量、矢量和张量扰动下光子测地线的一般修正，并计算其通过积分Sachs-Wolfe（ISW）效应对CMB的影响；重点分析各向异性应力瞬时注入的主导贡献。

Conclusion: 该形式化框架为约束各类晚期宇宙物理过程（如暗区相变）提供了通用、模型无关的工具，凸显了各向异性应力在CMB探测中的关键作用。

Abstract: We develop a formalism to characterize the imprints of late-time sources of cosmological fluctuations under the sole assumption that the injection occurs on timescales short compared to the horizon. For post-recombination injections, we derive the general modification of photon geodesics in the presence of scalar, vector, and tensor perturbations, and compute the resulting impact on the Cosmic Microwave Background through the integrated Sachs-Wolfe effect. We show that the signal is generically dominated by instantaneous injections of anisotropic stress. As an application, we consider first-order phase transitions in a sequestered dark sector and show that current observations constrain fractional energy injections at the permille level.
