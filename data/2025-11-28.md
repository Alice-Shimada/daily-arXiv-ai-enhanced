### [1] [Tidal forces around the Letelier-Alencar cloud of strings black hole](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.21604)
*Marcos V. de S. Silva,T. M. Crispim,R. R. Landim,Gonzalo Olmo,Diego Sáez-Chillón Gómez*

Main category: gr-qc

TL;DR: 本文研究了由弦云产生的广义Letelier-Alencar黑洞周围的相对论性潮汐力，分析了其曲率发散、测地线运动及潮汐效应，发现弦云参数显著影响光子层和稳定圆轨道半径，并改变潮汐力行为。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 探索弦云对黑洞时空结构和潮汐力的影响，扩展对广义相对论中奇异性和引力效应的理解。

Method: 基于广义Letelier-Alencar解计算Kretschmann标量，分析零测地线（光子轨道）和类时测地线（径向下落与圆轨道），并推导不同观测者的潮汐力表达式。

Result: 发现r→0处曲率发散强于Letelier和Schwarzschild情况；光子层和内最稳定圆轨道半径随gs增大而增大、随ls减小而减小；某些参数区域无圆轨道；径向下落中可能出现拉伸与压缩反转（通常在视界内）；圆周运动观测者的潮汐力受弦云影响且无符号变化。

Conclusion: 弦云参数显著改变黑洞周围几何结构与潮汐动力学，为观测信号提供新的理论特征。

Abstract: In this work, we investigate relativistic tidal forces around a black hole sourced by a cloud of strings, described by the generalized Letelier-Alencar solution. We first review the original Letelier spacetime and its recent generalization, computing the Kretschmann scalar, and showing that the generalized model exhibits a stronger curvature divergence at $r \to 0$ than both Letelier and Schwarzschild cases. We then analyze geodesic motion in this background. For massless particles, we focus on circular photon orbits, while for massive particles, we consider both radial infall and circular motion. We find that the radii of the photon sphere and of the innermost stable circular orbit increase with the cloud of strings parameter $g_s$ and decrease with the length scale $l_s$, and that circular orbits cease to exist in certain regions of the parameter space. For radial motion, we compute the radial acceleration and the corresponding tidal forces. In this case, we show that an inversion between stretching and compression may occur, although this regime is typically hidden inside the event horizon. Finally, we study tidal forces for observers in circular motion, showing that the cloud of strings modifies the Keplerian frequency and the tidal force profile even at large distances, and that in this case there is no sign change of the tidal components.

### [2] [A Super-Eddington, Lensing-Magnified Quasar at $z=5.07$ observed with JWST](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.21618)
*Katherine Panebianco,Minghao Yue,Anna-Christina Eilers,Xiaohui Fan,Feige Wang,Jinyi Yang,Robert A. Simcoe*

Main category: astro-ph.GA

TL;DR: 该研究利用JWST/NIRCam对红移z=5.07的类星体J0025-0145进行成像，结合HST数据，确认其未被前景星系多重成像，并未探测到宿主星系。通过SED拟合得出前景星系的质量和光度红移，支持单图像透镜模型，估计类星体最大放大倍数为3.2，其本征爱丁顿比率超过4.9，是目前已知高红移黑洞中最高的之一，表明早期宇宙中黑洞可能存在超爱丁顿增长。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 确定高红移类星体J0025-0145是否被引力透镜多重成像，并探究其本征性质，尤其是爱丁顿比率，以理解早期宇宙中超大质量黑洞的增长机制。

Method: 利用JWST/NIRCam的F070W和F480M波段成像，结合HST数据，分析点扩散函数（PSF）以判断是否存在多重成像；通过多波段测光拟合前景星系的能谱分布（SED），估算其质量和红移，并基于透镜模型估计类星体的最大放大倍数和本征爱丁顿比率。

Result: 确认J0025-0145为单图像透镜系统，未发现宿主星系；前景星系质量为log(M*/M☉) = 11.15±0.16，光度红移z_phot=3.62_{-0.04}^{+0.06}；类星体最大放大倍数μ_max=3.2，本征爱丁顿比率λ_Edd^intrinsic > 4.9。

Conclusion: J0025-0145是目前已知z>5类星体中爱丁顿比率最高的之一，支持早期宇宙中黑洞可通过超爱丁顿吸积快速增长的观点。

Abstract: We present JWST/NIRCam F070W and F480M imaging for a quasar at $z = 5.07$, J0025-0145, which is magnified by a foreground lensing galaxy. Existing Hubble Space Telescope (HST) imaging does not have sufficient spatial resolution to determine whether the background quasar is multiply imaged. Exploiting the sharp PSF of the F070W band, we confirm that the background quasar can be well-described by a single point spread function (PSF), essentially ruling out the existence of multiple lensed images. We do not detect the quasar host galaxy in either the F070W or the F480M band. Using the HST and JWST photometry, we fit the Spectral Energy Distribution (SED) of the foreground galaxy. The estimated mass ($\log(M_{*} / M_{\odot}) = 11.15 \pm 0.16$) and redshift ($z_{\text{phot}} = 3.62_{-0.04}^{+0.06}$) of the foreground galaxy are consistent with a single-image lensing model. We estimate the maximum possible magnification of the quasar to be $μ_{\text{max}} = 3.2$, which implies that the intrinsic Eddington ratio of the quasar is at least $λ_{\text{Edd}}^{\text{intrinsic}} > 4.9$. Therefore, J0025-0145 has one of the highest Eddington ratios among $z>5$ supermassive black holes known so far, suggesting the viability of super-Eddington growth for supermassive black holes in the early universe.

### [3] [Evidence For A Correlation Between Astrophysical Neutrinos and Radio Flares](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.21627)
*Yjan A. Gordon,Peter S. Ferguson,Eric J. Hooper,Michael N. Martinez*

Main category: astro-ph.GA

TL;DR: 利用VLASS和IceCube数据，发现射电变源与天体物理中微子之间存在显著关联，射电耀斑可能贡献约13%的IceCube中微子，且多数无γ射线或X射线对应体。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 寻找射电波段变源与高能中微子事件之间的关联，以识别中微子的电磁对应体并理解其起源。

Method: 结合VLASS前两期射电观测与IceCube中微子数据，统计分析射电耀斑与中微子在时间和空间上的关联性。

Result: 在>2σ置信度下发现射电耀斑与中微子存在超量关联，表明射电耀斑可能贡献约13%的天体物理中微子；超过80%的关联耀斑无γ射线或X射线辐射；无时间约束时未见整体射电变源与中微子的超量关联。

Conclusion: 射电波段在识别中微子对应体方面具有独特重要性，未来VLASS更多数据有望将关联性提升至>3σ甚至>5σ置信度。

Abstract: We use data from the first two epochs of the Very Large Array Sky Survey (VLASS) and the IceCube Neutrino Observatory to search for evidence of a correlation between radio variability and the detection of astrophysical neutrinos. We find an excess number of associations between flaring radio sources and neutrinos that were detected between the first and second VLASS observations at $>2σ$ confidence. This excess is consistent with radio flares contributing $\sim13\,\%$ of the astrophysical neutrinos observed by IceCube. Notably $>80\,\%$ of the radio flares associated with neutrinos are not detected at either $γ$-ray or X-ray wavelengths, highlighting the importance of radio observations for identifying potential electromagnetic counterparts to astrophysical neutrinos. No excess in the number of associations between the wider radio-variable population and the IceCube neutrinos is seen when no time constraint is placed on the neutrino detection. We predict that data from future VLASS epochs will see an excess number of associations between radio flares and neutrinos at the $>3σ$ level, and expected improvements to the positional constraints on the neutrinos may increase that confidence to $>5σ$, should our results be representative.

### [4] [Cosmological Probes of Lepton Parity Freeze-in Dark Matter: $ΔN_{\rm eff}$ & Gravitational Waves](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.21634)
*Ernest Ma,Partha Kumar Paul,Narendra Sahu*

Main category: hep-ph

TL;DR: 该论文提出在标准型I类中微子质量跷跷板机制中，引入具有偶数轻子数的马约拉纳费米子S作为暗物质候选者，并通过引入奇数轻子数的单态标量σ实现暗物质产生机制。根据再加热温度的不同，暗物质可通过右手中微子衰变或标准模型希格斯粒子衰变产生。该框架可导致强一阶电弱相变并产生额外相对论自由度，为未来引力波和CMB实验提供可观测信号。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 探索暗物质与中微子质量生成机制之间的联系，在保持轻子数对称性的框架下构建自然稳定的暗物质候选者，并解释当前宇宙观测中的未解问题如额外相对论自由度。

Method: 扩展标准型I类跷跷板模型，引入具有特定轻子数的马约拉纳费米子S和单态标量σ，分析不同参数区域下暗物质的产生机制（右手中微子衰变、希格斯衰变、σ晚期衰变），研究其对电弱相变的影响以及对ΔN_eff和引力波信号的贡献。

Result: 成功构建了一个兼容中微子质量和暗物质的理论框架，暗物质质量范围为MeV到TeV；预测了两种主要的暗物质产生途径；发现大σ-h耦合可引发强一阶电弱相变，小耦合下σ冻结后衰变可增加ΔN_eff；同时预言可观测的引力波信号。

Conclusion: 该模型统一描述了中微子质量和暗物质，并提供了可通过未来引力波和CMB实验检验的独特观测特征，包括ΔN_eff变化和强一阶相变产生的引力波，增强了粒子物理与宇宙学观测之间的联系。

Abstract: In the canonical type-I seesaw mechanism for neutrino masses, a residual symmetry known as lepton parity: $(-1)^L$, remains preserved. Introducing a Majorana fermion $S$ with even lepton parity renders it naturally stable, making it a viable dark matter (DM) candidate. The addition of a lepton parity odd singlet scalar $σ$ allows for the coupling $N S σ$, where $N$ is the right-handed neutrino. If $S$ is not thermalized, then DM relic can be produced in two distinct ways: (i) for reheating temperature, $T_{\rm rh}>m_{N}$, dominantly through the decay of $N$ ($N\rightarrow Sσ$), and (ii) for $T_{\rm EW}<T_{\rm rh}\ll m_{N}$, via standard model Higgs ($h$) decay ($h\rightarrow SS$ at one loop). If the $σ-h$ quartic coupling is large, then it can lead to a strong first-order electroweak phase transition even if $\langleσ\rangle=0$. Alternatively, if $σ-h$ coupling is small, then $σ$ can freeze out with a larger abundance, and hence its decay ($σ\rightarrow Sν$) at late epochs can give rise to additional relativistic degrees of freedom ($Δ{N}_{\rm eff}$). Thus, the framework gives a viable DM with mass range varying from MeV to TeV and leaves observable imprints, via gravitational waves and $Δ{N}_{\rm eff}$, which offer complementary probes, potentially detectable in future gravitational wave and CMB experiments.

### [5] [Updated bounds on the (1,2) neutrino oscillation parameters after first JUNO results](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.21650)
*Francesco Capozzi,Eligio Lisi,Francesco Marcone,Antonio Marrone,Antonio Palazzo*

Main category: hep-ph

TL;DR: 本文在标准3ν框架下，结合SNO+和JUNO最新实验结果，更新了中微子振荡参数δm²和sin²θ₁₂的全球约束，显著减小了其不确定性，并讨论了未来可能解除质量顺序简并性的物理与统计效应。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 精确测定中微子振荡参数δm²和sin²θ₁₂对于理解中微子质量和混合机制至关重要，而现有测量仍存在简并性问题，需借助最新实验数据进行更新和优化。

Method: 在2024年全球数据分析基础上，引入SNO+的最新约束以及JUNO的首批实验结果，重新拟合δm²和sin²θ₁₂的中心值与误差，并分析其相关性及未来可能影响参数估计的物理与统计因素。

Result: 截至2025年的更新结果显示：δm²/10⁻⁵ eV² = 7.48 ± 0.10（1σ误差1.3%），sin²θ₁₂ = 0.3085 ± 0.0073（1σ误差2.4%），两者相关系数为-0.20；JUNO数据显著降低了参数不确定性。

Conclusion: 结合SNO+和JUNO数据后，δm²和sin²θ₁₂的测量精度大幅提升，为未来解决中微子质量顺序简并性提供了更坚实的基础。

Abstract: Within the standard $3ν$ framework, we discuss updated bounds on the leading oscillation parameters related to the $(ν_1,\,ν_2)$ states, namely, the squared mass difference $δm^2=m^2_2-m^2_1$ and the mixing parameter $\sin^2θ_{12}$. A previous global analysis of 2024 oscillation data estimated $δm^2$ and $\sin^2θ_{12}$ with fractional $1σ$ errors of about $2.3\%$ and $4.5\%$, respectively. First we update the analysis by applying the latest SNO+ constraints, that slightly shift the $(δm^2,\,\sin^2θ_{12})$ best fits. Then we apply the constraints placed by the first JUNO results, that significantly reduce the uncertainties of both parameters. Our updated global bounds (as of 2025) can be summarized as: $δm^2/10^{-5}{\rm eV}^2 = 7.48\pm 0.10$ and $\sin^2θ_{12}=0.3085\pm0.0073$ (with correlation $ρ=-0.20$), corresponding to $1σ$ uncertainties as small as $1.3\%$ and $2.4\%$, respectively. We also comment on minor physical and statistical effects that, in the future, may contribute to lift the current mass-ordering degeneracy of $(δm^2,\,θ_{12})$ estimates.
