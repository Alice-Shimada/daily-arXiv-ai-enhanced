### [1] [A PANORAMIC of UV-optical morphologies of "Little Red Dots": Two groups of LRDs distinguished by UV half-light radius](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.24700)
*Aidan P. Cloonan,Katherine E. Whitaker,Sinclaire M. Manning,Christina C. Williams,Jenny E. Greene,Pascal A. Oesch,Andrea Weibel,Gabriel Brammer,Anna de Graaff,Raphael E. Hviding,Pratika Dayal,Christian Kragh Jespersen,Zhiyuan Ji,Ivo Labbe,Mengyuan Xiao,Yunchong Zhang*

Main category: astro-ph.GA

TL;DR: 本文利用JWST的PANORAMIC巡天数据，发现早期宇宙中的'小红点'(LRDs)在静止紫外波段比光学波段更延展，且在巴耳末断处发生形态转变；结果支持其紫外辐射来自恒星与围绕AGN的低尘氢云共同贡献，并暗示不同黑洞种子质量可能导致LRD紫外尺度差异。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: LRDs在早期宇宙中广泛存在但其紫外与光学辐射的主导机制（恒星形成 vs AGN）尚不明确，需通过多波段形态学分析厘清物理起源。

Method: 基于PANORAMIC巡天的大样本光度选源LRDs，结合单Sérsic拟合与PSF+Sérsic联合建模，分析其静止UV至光学波段的形态演化；辅以巴耳末断处的分辨性分组及堆叠分析，并验证于光谱样本。

Result: LRDs静止UV光分布普遍比光学更延展；巴耳末断处发生显著收缩（R50, opt ≲ 100 pc）；不可分辨子样本在全UV-光学波段均紧凑（R50 ≲ 100 pc），而可分辨子样本UV延展（R50, UV > 200 pc）；光谱样本呈现相同二分性。

Conclusion: LRDs的静止UV辐射由恒星与低尘、致密氢云包层中的AGN共同主导；UV尺度差异可能反映原初黑洞种子质量的多样性。

Abstract: Among the most remarkable results from JWST is the discovery of abundant, compact, and very red sources in the early Universe known as "Little Red Dots" (LRDs). The relative degree to which starlight and active galactic nuclei (AGN) drive the rest-frame UV and optical emission from LRDs remains unclear. With a large sample of LRDs selected photometrically from the pure-parallel PANORAMIC survey, we study their morphology as a function of rest-wavelength and find that the rest-UV light is typically more extended than the rest-optical. This result holds both when measuring LRD sizes with a single Sérsic profile and when comparing the fraction of light from a point source via joint PSF+Sérsic modeling. A shift occurs at the Balmer break, with LRDs becoming highly compact and unresolved ($R_{50,\rm{opt}}\lesssim100\;\rm{pc}$) in the rest-optical relative to the rest-UV. When splitting the sample at the Balmer break into those that are resolved and unresolved, a stacking analysis demonstrates that the latter are compact ($R_{50}\lesssim100\;\rm{pc}$) on average across the full rest-UV-optical spectrum. Conversely, those LRDs resolved at the break show extended UV emission ($R_{50,\rm{UV}}>200\;\rm{pc}$) on average. We find a similar dichotomy when repeating with a spectroscopic sample. Altogether, these results are consistent with the rest-UV emission driven by a combination of emission from starlight and a dense, dust-poor cloud of hydrogen gas enveloping an AGN. Differences between LRDs in the relative contribution from the AGN and starlight could reflect an ensemble of black hole seed masses, where a heavier seed produces an LRD of smaller $R_{50,\rm{UV}}$.

### [2] [Topological signatures in Kerr-Sen AdS black hole thermodynamics](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.24686)
*Mohd Rehan,Md Sabir Ali,Sushant G. Ghosh*

Main category: gr-qc

TL;DR: 本文利用Duan拓扑流理论和一种新颖的复留数方法，对异质弦理论中的Kerr-Sen AdS黑洞进行了拓扑研究，发现其存在小、中、大三种热力学相，对应总拓扑荷W=+1，且该拓扑数对胀子电荷参数不变，但依赖于角动量参数；并提出基于复平面上离壳Gibbs自由能的解析延拓新方法，用奇点留数直接编码绕数。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 建立坐标无关的黑洞相变理解框架，结合黑洞热力学与拓扑学，探索其普适性及对基本参数（如 dilaton 电荷、角动量、宇宙常数）的依赖性。

Method: 结合Duan拓扑流理论与新提出的复留数方法，构建广义离壳自由能，在参量空间中分析相应矢量场，定位零点并计算绕数；同时对离壳Gibbs自由能进行复平面解析延拓，利用孤立奇点的留数提取绕数信息。

Result: 发现Kerr-Sen AdS黑洞具有三个热力学相（小、中、大黑洞），对应绕数分别为+1、−1、+1，总拓扑荷W=+1；该拓扑荷对dilaton电荷不变，但依赖于转动参数；在a=0（GMGHS AdS）和Λ=0（渐近平直Kerr-Sen）极限下系统验证了该结论；复平面上的留数精确复现绕数。

Conclusion: 拓扑方法为黑洞相变提供了鲁棒、坐标无关的刻画；总拓扑荷W=+1是Kerr-AdS、RN-AdS及Kerr-Sen AdS黑洞共有的稳定拓扑特征，暗示深层普适性；旋转是驱动多临界点相结构的关键物理参数；复留数方法为热力学拓扑分析提供了新解析工具，并可能关联全息对偶。

Abstract: Black hole thermodynamics and topology have emerged as a strong foundation for a coordinate-independent understanding of phase transitions. Using both Duan's topological current theory and a novel complex residue method, we perform a topological study of the Kerr-Sen AdS black hole arising in heterotic string theory. In turn, we find the zero points corresponding to on-shell black hole states and calculate their winding numbers to find the global topological charge by building the generalized off-shell free energy and examining the corresponding vector field in a parametric space. Our analysis reveals that the Kerr-Sen AdS black hole exhibits three distinct thermodynamic phases -- small, intermediate, and large black hole branches -- characterized by critical points with winding numbers $+1$, $-1$, and $+1$ respectively, culminating in a total topological charge $W = +1$. Significantly, this topological number remains invariant under variations of the dilaton charge parameter, indicating that the dilaton field does not alter the fundamental topological class established for Kerr-AdS and RN-AdS black holes. However, the rotation parameter proves crucial in determining the phase structure and the emergence of multiple critical points. We systematically examine three limiting configurations: the full Kerr-Sen AdS spacetime, the GMGHS AdS limit ($a = 0$), and the asymptotically flat Kerr-Sen case ($Λ= 0$). In addition, we propose a novel approach that analytically continues the thermodynamic characterisation into the complex plane. The characterized complex function, derived from the off-shell Gibbs free energy, possesses isolated singular points whose residues directly encode the winding numbers. Our results indicate that topology offers deep insights into black hole phase transitions, with potential implications to holographic dualities.

### [3] [Heavy quark masses from step-scaling](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.25295)
*Simon Kuberski,Alessandro Conigli,Patrick Fritzsch,Antoine Gérardin,Jochen Heitger,Gregorio Herdoíza,Carlos Pena,Hubert Simma,Rainer Sommer*

Main category: hep-lat

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种利用重夸克步进缩放策略确定粲夸克和底夸克质量的新方法，通过小体积中的重整化与大体积CLS系综连接，并在不同尺度间插值得到高精度结果。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 传统大体积方法存在系统误差，需发展互补的、系统误差来源不同的新方法来提高夸克质量测定的精度与可靠性。

Method: 采用重夸克步进缩放（heavy-quark step-scaling）策略，在小体积中直接模拟相对论性底夸克并完成重整化；通过一系列有限体积模拟连接至大体积CLS系综；在除最小体积外的其他体积中，通过相对论性重夸克数据与静态极限之间的插值达到B标度。

Result: 获得了高精度的粲夸克与底夸克质量，系统误差较小且与标准大体积方法不同，具有互补性。

Conclusion: 该方法为强子物理中基本参数的精确测定提供了新途径，验证了步进缩放策略在重夸克质量计算中的有效性与优势。

Abstract: We present a determination of the charm- and bottom-quark masses using the heavy-quark step-scaling strategy. Renormalization is performed in small volumes where relativistic bottom quarks can be simulated directly. A sequence of finite-volume simulations connects this calculation to large-volume CLS ensembles, where simulations at physical light and strange quark masses provide reliable control over low-energy hadronic physics. In all but the smallest volume, the B-scale is reached by interpolating between relativistic heavy-quark data and the static limit. The resulting quark masses are obtained with good precision, with subdominant systematic uncertainties that differ from, and thus complement, those of standard large-volume determinations.

### [4] [The Vasiliev Grassmannian](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.24656)
*Shounak De,Hayden Lee*

Main category: hep-th

TL;DR: 本文将de Sitter空间中最小Vasiliev高自旋引力的标量四点函数表示为正交Grassmann流形OGr(4,8)上的积分，并发现其完全交叉对称形式与Veneziano振幅的场论极限一致，尽管源自高自旋无质量塔的无张力极限。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 探索高自旋引力在de Sitter空间中的散射振幅结构，建立与弦论振幅（如Veneziano振幅）的形式类比，揭示不同理论极限下的共性。

Method: 将标量四点函数表达为正交Grassmannian OGr(4,8)上的积分；引入Grassmannian Mandelstam变量S、T、U；通过围道积分计算并比对动量空间结果；直接在Grassmannian空间中分析奇点与留数。

Result: 获得四点函数的全交叉对称表达式(S²+T²+U²)/(STU)，其形式与Veneziano振幅场论极限相同；验证了该公式与动量空间已知结果一致；明确了其在Grassmannian空间中的奇点结构与物理极点对应关系。

Conclusion: Vasiliev高自旋引力在de Sitter空间的四点函数具有简洁优美的Grassmannian几何表示，且展现出与弦论振幅意外的结构统一性，暗示高自旋理论与弦论之间存在深层联系。

Abstract: We express the scalar four-point function of minimal Vasiliev higher-spin gravity in de Sitter space as an integral over the orthogonal Grassmannian OGr(4,8). The full crossing-symmetric Vasiliev Grassmannian correlator is given by $(S^2+T^2+U^2)/(STU)$, where $S$, $T$, $U$ are the Grassmannian Mandelstam variables. Remarkably, this has the same form as the field-theory limit of the Veneziano amplitude, despite arising from the opposite, tensionless limit of an infinite massless higher-spin tower. We verify the formula by evaluating the Grassmannian contour integral and matching it to the momentum-space result, and analyze its singularities and residues directly in Grassmannian space.

### [5] [Dual gravities from entanglement entropy](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.25270)
*Jaehyeok Huh,Chanyong Park*

Main category: hep-th

TL;DR: 本文利用基于规则的全息方法，从量子场论（特别是纠缠熵）数据中重建对偶引力理论，成功从二维热态的纠缠熵导出三维黑洞几何，并进一步提取热力学量；对于未变形CFT，通过Abel变换解析重构对偶几何；对相关变形CFT，则基于数值纠缠熵数据实现数值重建，并从中提取RG流相关物理量如β函数和c函数。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 从QFT数据（尤其是纠缠熵）出发，无需先验引力假设，实现对偶引力理论的自洽重建，拓展全息原理的应用范围并深化对AdS/CFT对应的理解。

Method: 采用规则化的全息方法，结合Abel变换解析重构、数值拟合与热力学分析，分别处理未变形CFT的解析情形和相关变形CFT的数值情形。

Result: 成功从纠缠熵重建三维黑洞几何并精确提取热力学量；实现未变形CFT下对偶几何的解析构造；完成相关变形CFT下对偶引力的数值重建，并从中提取β函数和c函数等RG流信息。

Conclusion: 纠缠熵蕴含足够信息以系统重建对偶引力理论，包括几何结构及更深层的RG流动力学，验证了全息熵-几何对应的有效性与普适性。

Abstract: Applying a rule-based holographic method, we investigate the reconstruction of dual gravity theories from the quantum field theory (QFT) data, specifically entanglement entropy. We first derive a three-dimensional black hole geometry from the entanglement entropy of a two-dimensional thermal system. Using the reconstructed solution, we extract various thermodynamic quantities with small numerical errors. Moreover, we explore how to reconstruct the dual gravity theory beyond the geometry itself. For an undeformed conformal field theory (CFT), we show that the dual gravity theory can be constructed analytically from the analytic form of the entanglement entropy. In particular, we demonstrate how to reconstruct the analytic dual geometry by applying the Abel transformation. Finally, we investigate the numerical reconstruction of the dual gravity theory from numerical entanglement entropy data for a relevantly deformed CFT. After reconstructing the dual gravity, we show that additional information about the renormalization group (RG) flow, for instance, the $\b$-function and the $c$-function, can be extracted for the considered relevantly deformed CFT.
